# DxFeed account sync

You can now link a DxFeed account straight from the dashboard **Import** flow. Sign in with your DxFeed email and password, pick the account to sync, and trades come in like other connected brokers.

This is for anyone who clears or routes through DxFeed and wants the same automatic refresh as Rithmic or Tradovate, without exporting files by hand.
