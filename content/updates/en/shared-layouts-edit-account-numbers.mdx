# Shared layouts: edit which accounts appear

In **Shared layouts** (where you manage public links to your dashboard), each card now lets you open an **accounts** editor. Pick from account numbers that already exist in your trades, add or remove them, and save.

That way a coach, investor, or teammate sees the right subset when you rotate accounts or fix a mistake, without generating a brand-new link.
