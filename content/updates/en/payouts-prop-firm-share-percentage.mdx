# Payouts: prop firm share percentage

When you add or edit a payout in **Accounts**, you can enter a **prop firm sharing** percentage. That is the slice the firm keeps before money hits your pocket.

Paid and validated payouts now roll into account and summary **total payouts** using the net amount after that share, so headline numbers reflect your take instead of the gross wire amount.
