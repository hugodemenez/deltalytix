# A clearer landing page, from hero to pricing

![Redesigned landing page with a bold hero and numbered feature sections](/updates/pr-298/en/landing-page-redesign.png)

The public homepage now presents Deltalytix in a clearer editorial flow. A bold hero, mint-framed product previews, partner logos, and numbered feature sections guide you from AI journaling and performance charts through to pricing, FAQs, and the open-source project.

Typography, spacing, colors, and section dividers have been refined across light and dark themes, on desktop and mobile. Key buttons are easier to tap and give clear feedback when pressed. The FAQ is now a readable accordion with fuller answers in both English and French.

![Expanded FAQ answer in the redesigned English accordion](/updates/pr-298/en/landing-page-redesign-faq.png)

Pricing comparisons are steadier too. Switch between **Monthly**, **Yearly**, and **Lifetime** on the homepage or pricing page, and the Basic and Plus cards stay in place while the details update. Explore the full redesign at `/en` or `/fr`.
