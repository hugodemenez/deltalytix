# Landing preview shows the AI coach in action

The **AI-Powered Journaling** block on the [homepage](/en) is no longer a static mockup. It now plays through a realistic coach conversation so visitors can see how journaling with AI works before signing in.

The demo types a suggested question, shows a brief thinking state, streams the coach response, and surfaces insight cards such as **Overall Win Rate** or **Revenge Trading Impact** with a short explanation underneath. It loops through several example prompts, respects reduced-motion preferences, and includes a reset control to replay the sequence.

![Staged AI coach demo with an insight card on the landing page](/updates/pr-316/en/landing-ai-coach-demo.png)

This preview complements the in-dashboard AI coach: it explains the workflow on the public site, while signed-in traders still get data-aware conversations tied to their own trades and journal entries.

[See the live demo in the features section](/en#ai-journaling).
