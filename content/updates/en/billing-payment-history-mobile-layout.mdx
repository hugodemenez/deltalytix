# Billing: payment history fits on mobile

![Payment history section on the billing page at mobile width](/updates/pr-298/en/billing-payment-history-mobile-layout.png)

The **Payment history** section on the billing page now stacks cleanly on phones. Amount and date sit above the status badge and action buttons, and **View Invoice** / **Download PDF** go full-width instead of spilling past the card edge. Desktop layout is unchanged.
