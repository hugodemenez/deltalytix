# Active filters live in the top bar

On desktop, the chips that show your current **accounts**, **instruments**, **date range**, tags, and the rest now sit in the **main navbar** next to the logo, instead of only down in the bottom toolbar.

You still get the same scroll and clear actions; they are just easier to spot while you move around the dashboard, and the lower toolbar stays focused on chart controls and layout tools.
