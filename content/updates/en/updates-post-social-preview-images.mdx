# Update posts show the right image when shared

When you share a link to a specific **Updates** article—on Slack, iMessage, X, LinkedIn, or elsewhere—the preview card should now include the **cover image** for that post instead of a broken or generic thumbnail.

We stopped hand-building preview image URLs that did not resolve correctly. Each update page now supplies its own share image automatically, so the title, description, and image in the card match the article you linked.
