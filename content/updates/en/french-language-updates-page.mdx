# French works on the Updates page again

If you picked **French** in the language menu, the **Updates** page could still show English posts and labels. That is fixed: opening **Updates** in French loads the French changelog entries, including the completed timeline and page labels in your language.

Your language choice also **sticks** when you hop between pages. The locale cookie is now set for the whole site, and signing in no longer wipes it. Paths like `/updates` are no longer forced to English before the app can read your preference—so French (and your other languages) stay consistent outside the dashboard too.
