# Subscription Plans

We're excited to introduce our new subscription model! We've carefully designed our plans to provide the right features at the right price point for every trader.

## Available Plans

### Basic Plan (Free)
- 1 trading account
- 1 week of data storage
- CSV imports
- Basic charts (Calendar & Equity)
- Perfect for trying out Deltalytix

### Plus Plan
- Unlimited accounts
- Unlimited data storage
- Rithmic direct connection
- All statistics and charts
- For serious traders

### Pro Plan
- Everything in Plus
- PnL correlation analysis
- Custom development
- Advanced analytics
- For professional traders
