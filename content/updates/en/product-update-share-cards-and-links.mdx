# Product update posts spell the name right and share cleanly

The **changelog** pages under **Updates** (each release note you read on the marketing site) now surface **Deltalytix** consistently in structured data—no more scrambled spellings creeping into previews or snippets.

Canonical links and multilingual alternates resolve through the canonical site instead of brittle hard-coded guesses, which keeps search indexes and pasted links coherent.

Social platforms also receive **explicit preview banners** generated for each post, so when you paste an update URL you tend to pick up imagery that mirrors the headline instead of a blank fall-back thumbnail.
