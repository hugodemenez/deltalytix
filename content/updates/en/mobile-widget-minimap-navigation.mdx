# Jump between mobile widgets with a toolbar minimap

On phones, the **Widgets** carousel still works for nearby cards—but when your layout is long, you can jump farther without swiping through every full-screen card.

A compact stack of upcoming widgets now sits in the mobile toolbar (after the filters). Tap it to open the **Widget minimap**: miniature previews of the remaining cards in your layout. Select a thumbnail to scroll straight to that widget and close the overlay.

![Expanded Widget minimap overlay showing miniature Trade Performance, Calendar View, and Equity Chart thumbnails](/updates/pr-332/en/mobile-widget-minimap-navigation.png)

The collapsed stack shows up to three upcoming widgets (the current card is excluded). Motion follows your scroll direction when you move through the carousel and respects reduced-motion preferences.

This control appears on mobile and compact viewports only. [Open the dashboard](/en/dashboard) on a phone or narrow window to try the minimap on the **Widgets** tab.
