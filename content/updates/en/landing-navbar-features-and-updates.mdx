# Landing navbar: Features and Updates menus

![Landing page navbar with Features and Updates dropdown menus](/updates/pr-298/en/landing-navbar-features-and-updates.png)

The marketing navbar now helps you jump around the homepage without scrolling blindly. The **Features** menu links straight to AI journaling, performance charts, calendar, performance tracking, and data import. **Updates** opens the changelog and our YouTube channel. **Pricing** summarizes Basic and Plus plans with a link to the pricing section.

On mobile, the menu uses accordions for theme and language, locks page scroll while open, and smooth-scrolls to in-page anchors when you pick a section.
