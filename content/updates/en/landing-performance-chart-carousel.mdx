# Landing page: swipeable dashboard chart previews

![Swipeable chart carousel on the landing page features section](/updates/pr-298/en/landing-performance-chart-carousel.png)

Two feature sections on the homepage now show rotating chart galleries that mirror what you see in the dashboard. **Performance Visualization** and **Track Your Performance** cycle through equity curves, daily P&L, weekday and hourly breakdowns, trade distribution, and daily tick targets.

The carousel auto-advances with animated dot progress, pauses when you interact or when reduced motion is preferred, and supports swipe navigation on mobile. Tap a dot to jump straight to a specific chart.
