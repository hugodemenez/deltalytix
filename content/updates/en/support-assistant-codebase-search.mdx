# A smarter, faster support assistant

![Refreshed support assistant chat](/updates/pr-249/en/support-assistant-codebase-search.png)

The support assistant on the help page can now search Deltalytix's own documentation and product details before answering, so its replies are more grounded in how the app actually works.

The chat interface—both on the support page and the in-dashboard assistant—was rebuilt with cleaner message bubbles, smoother streaming, and reasoning that's easier to follow. Sending a question and reading the answer feels quicker and tidier.
