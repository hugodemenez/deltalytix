# Landing page: weekly repo activity graph

![Weekly contribution graph on desktop](/updates/pr-269/en/landing-contribution-graph-desktop.png)

The **Open and Transparent** section on the landing page now includes a GitHub-style activity graph for the public repository—redesigned as a **weekly** view instead of a daily heatmap or line chart.

![Weekly contribution graph on mobile](/updates/pr-269/en/landing-contribution-graph-mobile.png)

## What changed

- **One bar per week** across the full calendar year, with no horizontal scrolling on mobile
- **Quarter month labels** (Jan, Apr, Jul, Oct) that stay readable on small screens
- **Muted gray bars** for upcoming weeks so you can see the rest of the year at a glance
- **Year navigation** to browse activity from the repo’s first year through today
- **Tap any week** to open a detail panel with active days and contributors
- **main & beta only** — commits are deduplicated by SHA across both integration branches

Activity data is cached weekly to keep the landing page fast while staying reasonably up to date.
