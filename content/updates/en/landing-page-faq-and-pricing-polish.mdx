# Clearer FAQs and steadier pricing

The redesigned public site received a finishing pass across typography, spacing, colors, and interactions. Headings wrap more naturally, sections are easier to scan in light and dark themes, and key controls are more comfortable to use on mobile.

The **FAQ** is now a readable accordion with expanded answers, clearer separation between questions, and localized copy in English and French.

![Expanded answer in the redesigned English FAQ](/updates/pr-298/en/landing-page-faq-and-pricing-polish.png)

Pricing comparisons are steadier too. Switch between **Monthly**, **Yearly**, and **Lifetime**, and the Basic and Plus cards stay in place while their details update.
