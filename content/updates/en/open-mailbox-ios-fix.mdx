# Open Mailbox works on iOS

After requesting a magic link, tapping **Open Mailbox** on iOS now takes you to your inbox so you can find the email. Previously, iOS Mail could open a compose screen instead, which made the sign-in flow confusing.

If the inbox can't be opened automatically, you'll see a toast reminding you to check your email manually.
