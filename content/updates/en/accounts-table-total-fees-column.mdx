# Accounts table: total fees column

The accounts **table** view adds a **Total fee** column. It starts from your activation fee, and for monthly-style accounts it multiplies the monthly price by how long the account has been active (from first trade to last trade), so you get a rough lifetime cost in one place.

Group summaries add up the same numbers across accounts, which helps when you are comparing prop spends side by side with balances and payouts.
