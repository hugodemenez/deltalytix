# Trade table: better on mobile and on big accounts

The **trade review** table now has a compact layout tuned for phones, so columns and row actions stay readable instead of overflowing off the side of a small screen.

We also virtualize the rows, which means selecting **Show all** only renders what's on screen as you scroll. Large accounts with thousands of trades can open the full list without the page freezing or crashing.
