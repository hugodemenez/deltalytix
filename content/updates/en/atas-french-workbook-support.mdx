# ATAS imports support French journal exports

ATAS can export your journal in French. The importer now recognizes those column names—**Compte**, **Instrument**, **Heure d'ouverture**, **Prix de clôture**, and the rest—and maps them to the same fields as the English export.

It also accepts a sheet named **Journal commercial**, not only **Journal**. If your ATAS UI is in French, you can upload the workbook as-is from the ATAS import flow without renaming columns or sheets first.
