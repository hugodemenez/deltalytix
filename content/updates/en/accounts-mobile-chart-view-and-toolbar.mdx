# Accounts: mobile chart view with carousels and toolbar

![Mobile accounts view with group and account carousels](/updates/pr-288/en/accounts-mobile-chart-view-and-toolbar.png)

The **Accounts** tab on mobile now has a dedicated chart view built for small screens. Swipe horizontally through your account groups, then through the accounts inside each group—each card shows progress, balance, and key stats at a glance.

A bottom toolbar keeps everyday actions within thumb reach: search accounts, sort the list, switch between chart and table views, create a new group, or open the group editor. You no longer need a desktop to organize accounts on the go.
