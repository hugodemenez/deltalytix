# DxFeed: clearer connection list with trading accounts

Saved DxFeed logins now appear as **accordion cards** instead of a flat table. Each card shows the prop firm, the login email, token status, last sync, and a **Schedule** or **Edit** link for the daily sync time.

Expand a connection to see every **trading account** it covers—useful when one login syncs several evaluation accounts at once. The actions menu (**Sync**, **Reconnect**, **Remove**) closes properly after you pick an option.

Removing a connection no longer sounds like deleting your journal: the confirm dialog explains that **already imported trades stay** in Deltalytix. You can remove accounts or trades separately in Data Management if you want them gone.
